Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026 · Last updated: April 14, 2026

🔒 Our Core Promise

  • We use Apple HealthKit ONLY to display your heart rate on your screen.
  • We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your data to any servers.
  • We do NOT sell, share, or monetize your personal data with third parties.
  • We do NOT use any tracking or analytics frameworks.

1. Who We Are

Stillō ("the App") is developed and published by MSK Produkt. The App is a wellness journal, relaxation tool, and informational companion designed to help you track and manage everyday anxiety. Stillō is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice.

2. Data We Access

2.1 Apple HealthKit

With your explicit permission, Stillō may read:

  • Heart Rate — displayed on-screen during breathing exercises for informational purposes only.

With your explicit permission, Stillō may write:

  • Mindful Minutes — to record completed breathing sessions in Apple Health.

HealthKit Disclosure

  • HealthKit data is accessed only on your device.
  • HealthKit data is never uploaded to any server, cloud, or third-party service.
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising, analytics, or tracking.
  • HealthKit data is not linked to your identity.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time via Settings → Health → Stillō.

2.2 Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device in the App's sandboxed container:

  • Journal entries (mood, notes, triggers)
  • Mood tracking data
  • Breathing session history
  • Emergency contact names and phone numbers you add
  • App preferences (theme, language, notification settings)
  • Achievement progress

This data is never transmitted off your device. If you delete the App, all local data is permanently erased.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

Stillō does not collect, access, or process:

  • Your name, email, or account information (no sign-up required)
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV used only by Apple for subscriptions)
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Contacts (beyond what you voluntarily enter as emergency contacts)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data

4. Third-Party Services

Stillō uses no third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. The only external service is:

  • Apple StoreKit 2 — for in-app subscription management. Apple processes payments per their Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

6. Children's Privacy

Stillō is rated 12+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data Security

All data resides in the iOS/watchOS sandbox, protected by your device's encryption (Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode). We employ no additional servers or databases because we have no servers — your device is the only place your data lives.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since we do not collect personal data on any server, there is no data for us to access, port, correct, or delete on our side. All your data is controlled entirely by you on your device. You can:

  • Delete all data by deleting the App.
  • Revoke HealthKit access via iOS Settings at any time.
  • Cancel subscriptions via App Store → Subscriptions.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
